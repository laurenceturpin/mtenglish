I'm Morag.

an english teacher.


Hello! My name’s Morag and I am a native English speaker from the UK. I have the CELTA certificate and am also TESOL qualified. I have taught students from early teens to late seventies at all different levels of English. I have 14 years’ experience both in the classroom and online. I love to meet people from different countries and cultures. I like talking about literature, arts, society and travel as well as other topics. I am friendly and have a relaxed lesson style which will help you feel less nervous so that you can improve your English skills.

Me as a Teacher

I like to get to know my students as this helps me tailor the lesson to the student’s interests and needs. My lessons are designed to help you become more fluent. A focus on pronunciation will help you to sound natural and I have a standard neutral British accent. As my lesson style is relaxed you will feel more confident when speaking and I aim to let students speak as much as possible. I will correct grammar and pronunciation mistakes at the end of the lesson so that you can focus on these areas in order to progress. While I have been teaching online, I have used Skype and also Zoom extensively.

My Teaching Style

Assessment

First of all, I will assess your level so that I know where you need to improve. I prepare each lesson in advance and use a variety of materials to help you learn.

Tailored to you, for your personal improvement

We would go at your pace and focus on your difficulties with English. We can discuss topics that interest you and in so doing you will learn idiomatic expressions and also vocabulary used by people who speak English fluently.

Helpful real-life scenario work

Role-play is also useful and if you like we can act out situations such as job interviews, travelling, or eating out etc. If there are certain areas you would like to work on, please let me know.
